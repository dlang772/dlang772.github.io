Hi, my name is Dan. I enjoy deconstructing the science behind our thoughts and actions including why we might prefer one brand of product over another. I see an overlap between marketing and psychology, and want to use a scientific approach to creating new content.

I am also passionate about usability design and realize the growing importance of having an accessible interface. Having worked in the healthcare industry and dealt with lackluster ERM software, I want to find new ways to improve the workfow for healthcare providers - then they can spend more time doing what they are good at; helping people! In addition to designing better products, I am enthusiastic about helping improve people's lives through volunteer work at the Tufts Sharewood Clinic. Outside of class I'm an avid racer for the Tufts Cycling Team, amateur rock climber, and freelance photographer (click here to check out my photographs).
