Welcome to a peek into my life. My name is Daniel Lang. I enjoy deconstructing the science behind our thoughts and actions including why we might prefer one brand of product over another. I see an overlap between marketing and psychology, and want to use a scientific approach to creating new content.

I am also passionate about usability design and realize the growing importance of having an accessible interface. In addition to designing better products, I am enthusiastic about helping improve people's lives through volunteer work at the Tufts Sharewood Clinic. Outside of class I'm an avid racer for the Tufts Cycling Team, amateur rock climber, and freelance photographer.
